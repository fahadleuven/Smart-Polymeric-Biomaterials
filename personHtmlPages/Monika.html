Monika Papić

Monika has obtained summa cum laude Bachelor’s degree in Environmental Engineering with a focus on Bioengineering from Faculty of Chemical Engineering and Technology, University of Zagreb, Croatia - her home town.

In 2024 she obtained her Master’s degree with Honors in Chemical Engineering with a specialization track in Health Chemical Engineering from TU Delft, The Netherlands.

During her masters, she has designed and developed ion-selective electrodes for detection of ions from human sweat.

At the moment, Monika is pursuing her joint PhD at Ghent University (Polymer & Biomaterials Group) and KU Leuven designing a melanoma wound-specific dressing based on biopolymers with biosensor incorporation for specific detection of biomarkers.

She is focusing on the synthesis and optimization of dressing constituents through biopolymer modification, hybrid hydrogel network formation and electrospinning. Besides that, she is developing in-house fabricated low-cost electrodes for the purpose of biosensor’s further development.
