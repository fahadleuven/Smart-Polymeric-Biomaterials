Our Laboratory

Our laboratory focuses on advanced research in smart polymeric biomaterials, nanotechnology, and biomedical applications. We provide a collaborative and interdisciplinary environment for innovation and discovery.

Lab Overview

The Smart Polymeric Biomaterials Laboratory is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities for material synthesis, characterization, and biological evaluation. Our research bridges chemistry, materials science, and biomedical engineering.

Facilities & Equipment

Polymer Synthesis

Facilities for controlled polymerization and chemical modification.

Characterization

Advanced tools for structural, thermal, and mechanical analysis.

Biomedical Testing

Cell culture and in-vitro testing facilities for biomaterials.

Research Environment

Our lab encourages creativity, collaboration, and mentorship. Students and researchers work closely with faculty and international collaborators to tackle real-world biomedical challenges.
